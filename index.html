    Meet our team

    The Interactive Media Studio  in Chengdu University of Information Technology is an interdisciplinary 

    research lab that aims to create future media technologies and friendly interfaces.

    Not only lie in the augmented reality ,gestural interfaces , wearable computing, intelligence hardware and other technologies, but also focus on how to combine them with unique and meaningful themes to create unique experience of human-computer interactions.
